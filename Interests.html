Ensemble Stars

While some may say gaming is a waste of time, Ensemble Stars is a game that I believe is worth my time and effort. Have you ever had a game that makes you grind for a png of a fictional idol? Have you ever pulled on a gacha over 100 times for your best boy and him to not come home and force you to shed tears because you are a free to play player? Do you ever stay up at night before a calculus test thinking about [insert character]'s lore and cry about it? No? Well, for me, Emsemble Stars made me say yes to every question listed.

Ensemble Stars, or Enstars for short, is an idol gacha rhythm game where I tap tap my way towards sucess. Playing on both the Japanese and English server, I would say I am pretty decent, full comboing 95%+ expert beatmaps, including Neo Sanctuary, Distorted Heart, Exceed, Last Lament, and more. While to a non-enstarries, you may not know what that means, just know it is quite impressive. Some other facts about my Enstars journey: my favorite character is Hiyori Tomoe and will always be Hiyori. My close second fav is Tetora becuase I like that his hair looks like Shadow the Hedgehog. I also happen to own a Yuzuru Fushimi nui (plushie), which I take very good care of (pictured below).

As a whole, through Ensemble Stars' unique lore, it has taught me lessons about self-confidence, loving myself, how to not commit a war crime, and friendship. I would 10/10 reccomend this game to any person who loves music, anime, and pretty people.
